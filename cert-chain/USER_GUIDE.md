# Cert-Chain User Guide

## Introduction
Cert-Chain allows you to cryptographically issue and verify credentials using the Stellar blockchain.

## For Institutions (Issuers)
1. **Connect Wallet:** Use the "Connect Freighter" button in the navigation bar.
2. **Navigate to Issue Tab:** Fill in the Student Name, Email, Course, and the name of your Institution.
3. **Mint:** Click **Issue Certificate**. Sign the transaction in your Freighter Wallet.
4. **Result:** The system will permanently mint the certificate on-chain, save a backup locally, and send an automated email to the student with their Hash!

## For Students / Employers (Verifiers)
1. **Receive the Hash:** The student shares their Certificate TX Hash or the provided QR Code.
2. **Open the Verify Tab:** No wallet connection is required!
3. **Paste & Verify:** Enter the hash and click "Verify Certificate".
4. **Result:** The immutable blockchain ledger is queried, and the pristine certificate details are presented.

## Viewing Records
The **Records Tab** and **Dashboard Tab** present real-time insights and a full history of credentials issued. Use the dashboard chart to identify trends.
